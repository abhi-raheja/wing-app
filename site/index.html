Features built for serious research

Wing It

Save any page with a click. Wing captures URL, title, and favicon, then generates a concise summary.

Highlights + Notes

Highlight key passages on saved pages and add notes. Highlights restore automatically.

Ask AI

Query your saved pages in natural language and get cited answers tied to the sources you saved.

Smart Connections

Discover related pages based on semantic similarity, shared collections, and domains.

Organize Deeply

Collections and nests let you build a hierarchy that matches your actual mental model.

Local‑First

Your data stays in your browser. Export or import anytime. No backend, no tracking.

How it works

1

Privacy‑first by design

Your data stays on your device. Wing uses your API key to talk directly to Anthropic or OpenAI for summaries and queries. No Wing servers, no tracking, no data collection.
