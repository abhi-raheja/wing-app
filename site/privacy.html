Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2, 2026

Overview

Wing is a Chrome extension that helps you save and organize web pages with optional AI‑powered summaries, connections, and query features. This policy explains what data Wing processes and how it is handled.

Data We Process

  • Saved pages (“wings”): URL, title, favicon, timestamp, and optionally page content for AI summarization.
  • Organization data: collections, nests, tags, and relationships between saved pages.
  • Highlights and annotations: selected text and your notes.
  • AI queries and responses: your prompts and generated answers.
  • API keys: stored locally so you can use your chosen AI provider.

How Data Is Used

Wing uses your data only to deliver product features you initiate, such as generating summaries, finding connections, restoring highlights, and answering questions.

Where Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB and Chrome storage. Wing does not operate a backend service and does not collect user data.

Third‑Party AI Providers

If you enable AI features, Wing sends relevant page content and/or your question to your selected AI provider (Anthropic or OpenAI) using your API key.

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you delete it or clear it using in‑product controls. You can export or import your data at any time.

Children’s Privacy

Wing is not intended for use by children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above.
